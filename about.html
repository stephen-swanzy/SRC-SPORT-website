About the academy

Building confident students through sport.

The SRC Sports Academy is a university-led initiative that connects students, coaches, parents, and community athletes through structured sports development.

Mission

To provide accessible, disciplined, and inclusive training that improves health, teamwork, leadership, and competitive performance.

Vision

To become a leading regional sports academy known for excellence, community engagement, and responsible athlete development.

Values

Respect, safety, fairness, accountability, consistency, and service to the university community.

Funding and Community Support

The academy is supported through SRC funding, university backing, community contributions, and future partnerships with sports bodies and sponsors.

Its structure allows future expansion into online payments, athlete dashboards, alumni networks, sponsorship pages, and competition archives.

Accessibility Features

Pages use semantic HTML, clear labels, keyboard-friendly links, ARIA labels where helpful, and strong contrast for readable content.
