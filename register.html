Before you join

Registration benefits

Registering gives you access to programmes, expert coaching, event updates, and academy support for every student.

  • Do I need experience? No, new and returning athletes are welcome in every sport.
  • What should I bring? Comfortable clothes, shoes, and a water bottle for training sessions.
  • How do I get updates? Subscribe to academy announcements during registration.
