Expanded programmes

New sports for the SRC community.

Wrestling, taekwondo, judo, and volleyball are being introduced to broaden student participation and attract community athletes.

Combat

Wrestling

Focus areas include stance, balance, controlled takedowns, safe mat movement, and tournament etiquette.

  • Level: beginner to competitive
  • Kit: training wear, water bottle
Martial Arts

Taekwondo

Sessions develop flexibility, kicking technique, reaction speed, discipline, and respectful sparring.

  • Level: beginner to intermediate
  • Kit: flexible clothing, towel
Martial Arts

Judo

Students learn falling safely, throws, hold-downs, body control, and the values of mutual welfare.

  • Level: beginner friendly
  • Kit: mat-safe clothing
Team Sport

Volleyball

Training covers serving, reception, teamwork, rotations, attack timing, and communication on court.

  • Level: social to competitive
  • Kit: court shoes recommended
