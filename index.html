Questions

Frequently asked questions

Do I need experience before joining?

No. Beginners and returning athletes are welcome. Coaches place each student at the right starting level.

What should I bring to training?

Bring comfortable sportswear, training shoes, water, and any sport-specific kit your coach requests.

Can I try more than one sport?

Yes. Students can explore multiple programmes before choosing a main training pathway.

How do I get updates?

Use the registration form, follow the academy socials, or contact the team through WhatsApp.

Need help?

Contact the academy desk

Use phone, email, or WhatsApp for booking support, venue directions, kit requirements, and programme advice.
